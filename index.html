    • About Me

    I am a graduating student majoring in Biology with a specialization in Microbiology and Immunology. Currently, I am working as a Research Assistant at the university lab where I have gained extensive experience in performing various laboratory techniques, including cell culture, PCR, and Western blotting. I have also contributed to multiple research projects and published a peer-reviewed article in a scientific journal. My ultimate goal is to become a medical doctor with a specialization in infectious diseases to make a positive impact on public health. In my free time, I enjoy volunteering at local clinics and reading medical journals to stay up-to-date on the latest advances in the field.
